# Offline Calibration

These materials are optional reference only. They are not part of the live review workflow.

## What They Are For

- remembering what kinds of risks mattered in past PD reviews
- checking whether the lane design still matches real maintainer concerns
- comparing future automation ideas against historical cases

## What They Are Not For

- choosing lanes automatically
- generating findings for the current PR
- copying historical comment wording into a live review

## Current Materials

- `benchmark-v1.json`
- `shadow-pr-corpus.jsonl`
- `evals/description-queries.json`
- `evals/review-cases.json`
- `scripts/validate_pd_pr_review.py`

Use them only when you are refining the review skill itself, not when you are reviewing a normal PR.
